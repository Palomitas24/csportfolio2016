Vindinium Project

What is Vindinium?

Vindinium is a game, where you have to control your character through programing it. It is also known as an Artifical Intellengence programing challenge. You have to take control of your hero, using the programing language of your choice. You will fight with other AI (other players) in a number of turns. You have to fight, capture gold mines, with sometimes with Goblins inside the mines protecting it, and survive by drinking cream soda. The hero, or player with the most coins collected, wins. If two player gets the same ammount of gold when the turns run out, there will be a draw and no one will win.
