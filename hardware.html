Hardware

Motherboard

The motherboard is a printed board contianing the main components of a computer, with connectors into which other circuits boards can be inserted.

History

The IBM, which invented most of the computer parts,invented the motherboard in 1981.It helped to provide a platform for the computer using RAM CPU,keyboards, and a mouse.

Central Processing Unit (CPU)

The Central Processing Unit is a part of the computer in which the operations are controlled and perform.

History

The CPU was intoduced by Intel in 1972. It was called the 8008 processer. Which it helped control of the operations of the computer.

Random-access memories (RAM)

Random access memories (RAM) is a form of computer data storage, allows data items to be read or written in almost the same amount of time.

History

Vacuum Tubes were used to be the RAMs back in the day. They had a diffrent concept of memory and did really looked like tubes.

Hard Drive

A hard drive is a high-capacity, self-contained storage device containing a read-write mechanism inside a sealed unit.

History

Invented in the 1950's by IBM, the frist hard drives used to be big and very heavy. They were also expensive. The hard drives were the size of a refrigerators.

BUS

If you think of a bus, you may think of the transportation vehicle. It's kinda like that. A BUS in computing is they connect the the internal components of a computer. The BUS intended to connect to local devices.

History

The BUS were first used in the IBM in the 1950's. They made it easier to connect to other parts of a computer, including the RAM and motherboard.

Keyboard

A keyboard is the set of typewriter-like keys that enables you to enter data into a computer or other devices. Computer keyboards are similar to electric-typewriters but contain additional typing keys.

History

Looking at the history of keyboard, they come back from the typewrites used back in the 1800's. But in the mid-1900's, the typewiter became more technologly adapted to computers.

Mouse

-
A computer mouse is an input device that issued with a personal computer. Items can be moved or selected by pressing the mouse buttons (called clicking).

History

The mouse was invented by Douglas Engelbart in 1964. And we are not talking about the animal. The mouse consisted of a wooden shell, circuit board and two metal wheels. It worked when it contact with the surface it was being used on.

Monitor

-
A monitor is the screen component of a computer, free standing. It looks like a TV (well almost).

History

The first monitors were made in 1970's to be compatable with the key board and mouses. The screen are made of cathode ray tubes and video display terminals.
